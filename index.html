Intro What we sell?

Explore kids and baby products galore at Valli Kids Shoppee, the Store for Little kids. We empathize with you as a mother who always aspires to bestow her newborn, infant, baby or kid with the best things even for their smallest needs. From breastfeeding to bathing essentials, right furniture to myriad baby care products, a mother needs them all for her lil' one.We sell bicycles of top class brands such as BSA, Hercules, Atlas and more and story books and colouring books from famous publications. This inspires us to offer convenient & hassle-free shopping not just for babies & kids but for moms-to-be & new moms as well.With top-notch national and international brands like Pampers, Mamypoko,Mee Mee, Huggies, Himalaya, FisherPrice, and more you can look forward to an unparalleled shopping experience with us.

Bicycles

We have got bicycles of various ranges for ages 3-40 years and there are of cycles which consist of tubeless tyres and which has no need to be pumped and it do not get punctured.

Battery-Operated Cars

Battery Powered Wheels Car RC are made to have your little one driving around in their own style. This battery-powered car that has a room for two and is ideal for kids ages 2-7 years.It is to be charged for once in 8 hours.
